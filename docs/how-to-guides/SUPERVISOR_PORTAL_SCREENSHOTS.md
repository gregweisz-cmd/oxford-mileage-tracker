## Before you start

1. Log in as a **supervisor** (user with supervisor role).
2. Open the **Supervisor Portal** (via portal switcher or direct link).
3. Use **Chrome or Firefox**, ~1920×1080, **100% zoom**.
4. Capture: **Win+Shift+S** (Windows) or **Cmd+Shift+4** (Mac). Save as PNG.
