Quick Start Guide

Get Started in 5 Minutes

This guide helps you use the Supervisor Portal to review and approve expense reports from your team. The portal has four tabs: Approvals, Reports, Team, and Analytics.

Logging In

Open the Oxford House Expense Tracker web portal in your browser and enter your email and password. The same login is used for all portals. The portal switcher only appears if your account is assigned access to multiple portal types—if you don't see it, you’re already being sent to the portal that matches your assigned access. If you think you should see the switcher and don’t, reach out to your administrator(s).

Viewing the Portal

After logging in, you'll see the Supervisor Portal with four tabs: Approvals (reports pending your review), Reports (all team reports with filters), Team (your team members), and Analytics (KPIs and expense trends). Use the Approvals tab to see what needs your review first.

Approving Your First Report

From the Approvals or Reports tab, click a report to open it (View or Details). Review the employee's mileage, receipts, hours, and descriptions. When satisfied, click Approve to send the report to the next step (e.g. Finance). If something needs to be fixed, use Request Revision and add comments so the employee can update and resubmit.

Comprehensive Reference

Portal Overview and Tabs

The Supervisor Portal header shows your name and a notification bell. The main content is organized into four tabs:

  • Approvals – Reports from your team that are pending your review. The tab may show a count (e.g. Reports (3)).
  • Reports – All reports from your team (any status). You can search by employee and filter by status and month.
  • Team – The list of employees who report to you (your direct reports). You can open an employee's report for a given month from here.
  • Analytics – Team performance KPIs and a 6‑month expense trend chart (pending reviews, approvals this month, average approval time, etc.).

Approvals Tab

The Approvals tab lists reports that are waiting for your review. Each row shows employee name, period (month/year), status, amount, miles, and actions (View, Details). Click View to open the report in full view or Details for the detailed report view. From there you can Approve or Request Revision. If there are no reports pending, you'll see a message saying so.

Reports Tab

The Reports tab shows all team reports regardless of status. Use the search box to find an employee and the filters to limit by status (e.g. Pending Supervisor, Approved, Needs Revision) and month. Open any report with View or Details to review or take action.

Team Tab

The Team tab lists the employees who have you as their supervisor. These are the people whose reports appear in Approvals and Reports. You can click an employee to view their report for a selected month. If your team list is empty, no one has been assigned to you as supervisor in Admin Portal > Employee Management.

Analytics Tab

The Analytics tab shows Team Performance Analytics: KPI cards (team members count, pending reviews, approvals this month, average approval time) and a 6‑month team expense trend chart. Use this tab to monitor your team's submission and approval patterns.

Approving a Report

1 Open the report

From Approvals or Reports, click View or the row to open the report, or Details for the detailed report view.

2 Review the report

Review mileage, receipts, hours, and descriptions. Expand sections and check the summary as needed.

3 Approve

Click Approve and confirm. The report moves to the next step (e.g. Finance). You may need to complete a certification before approving.

If you need changes from the employee, use Request Revision instead (see below). Some organizations allow you to Delegate a report to another supervisor.

Requesting Revisions

When something in a report needs to be corrected or clarified:

1 Open the report

Open it from Approvals or Reports as above.

2 Click Request Revision

Click Request Revision (or "Request Revision from Employee" in the dialog).

3 Enter comments

You must enter comments explaining what to change. You may be able to select specific items (mileage, receipts, time entries) to flag. The employee sees your comments, makes changes, and resubmits; the report then returns to your queue.

Where Reports Go After You Approve

When you approve a report:

  1. The report moves to the next step in the workflow (e.g. Finance for full monthly reports, or stops at Supervisor for weekly check-ups depending on configuration).
  2. Finance (or the next reviewer) can approve or request revisions.
  3. If revisions are requested, the report goes back to the employee; after they edit and resubmit, it may come back to you or go to Finance depending on the workflow.

Notifications

The notification bell in the header shows alerts such as new report submissions from your team, approval reminders, and system messages. Click the bell to open the notifications panel and review or clear items.

Profile Settings

Supervisors use the same profile and notification preferences as staff. There are no supervisor-specific settings; access your profile via the header (e.g. your name or a settings icon) to update your password or notification preferences if your organization allows it.

Tips & Best Practices

Review reports in the Approvals tab regularly so your team's reports move through the workflow on time. Use Request Revision with clear comments when something needs to be fixed; the employee can then edit and resubmit. The Analytics tab helps you spot backlog (pending reviews) and approval trends. If you can delegate, use the Delegate option when a report should be reviewed by another supervisor.

Tip

If your Team tab is empty, ask your administrator to assign employees to you as supervisor in Admin Portal > Employee Management or Supervisor Management.

Troubleshooting

No reports in Approvals

If Approvals is empty, either no one has submitted a report yet or reports have already been approved. Check the Reports tab with status filter "All" to see all team reports. Ensure employees have you set as their supervisor in the system.

Can't approve or request revision

Confirm you're logged in as a supervisor and that the report is in a status that allows your action (e.g. Pending Supervisor). If the button is disabled, the report may have moved to the next step or already been revised. Refresh the page and try again.

Portal slow or missing tabs

Refresh the page and check your connection. If you don't see Approvals, Reports, Team, or Analytics, confirm your role includes Supervisor access; contact your administrator if needed.

Still Having Issues?

Contact your system administrator or technical support with a description of the problem, steps you've tried, and any error messages or screenshots.
