# Beta Tester Packet

## Welcome

Thank you for being a Beta tester for the Oxford House Expense Tracker rollout.

Your mission:
- Use the app in real day-to-day scenarios.
- Focus on **usability and experience** (clarity, speed, workflow).
- Continue reporting bugs when you find them.

## Important: Keep Normal Expense Process

Do **not** use this app as a replacement for your normal expense workflow yet.

You must continue to:
- Track expenses the same way you do today.
- Submit expenses to your supervisor through the normal process.

## Access and Download

### iPhone (Primary Tester Path)
- Install **TestFlight** from the App Store: [Download TestFlight](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/testflight/id899247664)
- Goose will invite you to the app using your Apple ID/iCloud email.
- If your iCloud/TestFlight email is **not** your work email, tell Goose.

### Android
- Install from internal testing: [Join Android Internal Test](https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701544356101901260)
- If your Google Play email is **not** your work email, tell Goose.

### Web Portals
- Staff Web Portal: [Staff Portal Login](https://oxford-mileage-tracker.vercel.app/login)
- Supervisor Web Portal: [Supervisor Portal Login](https://oxford-mileage-tracker.vercel.app/login)

## Submit Feedback and Bugs

Use this form for every issue, improvement idea, or concern:

- [Submit Tester Feedback Form](https://forms.gle/vmUf6qzESDk5uTai8)

Please fill in as much detail as possible:
- Your Name
- Platform (`Web Portal` or `Mobile App`)
- Issue Type (`Bug`, `Enhancement`, `User Experience Improvement`, `Question/Concern`)
- Description (short)
- Summary (more detail)
- Steps to Reproduce
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Severity (`Critical`, `High`, `Medium`, `Low`)
- Frequency (`Always`, `Often`, `Sometimes`, `Once`)
- Upload screenshots/files when possible

## Beta Testing Focus Checklist

- End-to-end workflows feel intuitive and fast.
- Screen labels, prompts, and error messages are clear.
- Navigation between mobile and web features feels consistent.
- Common weekly tasks can be completed without confusion.
- Any friction points or “this could be easier” moments are reported.
