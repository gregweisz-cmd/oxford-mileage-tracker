Privacy Policy

Oxford House Expense Tracker
Effective date: March 19, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Oxford House, Inc. collects, uses, and shares information when employees and approved staff use the Oxford House Expense Tracker mobile app and web portal.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, work email, employee profile details)
  • Expense data (mileage entries, receipts, amounts, categories, notes)
  • Location data used for mileage tracking features
  • Usage and diagnostic data to support troubleshooting and reliability

How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality (tracking, reporting, approvals)
  • Sync and process expense report data
  • Support users and resolve technical issues
  • Meet legal, accounting, and organizational compliance requirements

How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the service and support expense workflows, including with authorized internal reviewers and service providers that host or maintain the platform. We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention and Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data. Information is retained according to operational and compliance needs.

Your Choices

Users can manage device permissions (such as location and camera access) in device settings and may manually enter certain data when supported by the app.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact support@oxfordhouse.org.
